This… this was disconcerting. He stepped to the window, looking out at the sprawling city of Elantris. His city. He had shaped it, molded it to his will. But now, he saw the beauty in the gardens, the vibrant colors of the marketplace, the smiles on the faces of the common folk. Things he had never truly *seen* before. He only saw opportunity.
